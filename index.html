About Scope

Scope is multi-faceted tool for making connections. It allows you to locate Emails, locate Linkedins, and gather Contacts. With Scope you will be able to reach and connect with experts in all types of fields. Go ahead and try!

Features

Our features include email trackers, website contact scrapers, and most importantly Linkedin account finders. Email trackers figure out the email of the person you are trying to reach. Website contact scrapers travel through a website and locate all numbers, emails, instagrams, etc. associated with the link.
